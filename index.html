Welcome Trainer!

So you think you have what it takes to be the very best like no one ever was, eh? We definitely hope so.

Professor Oak and his team of PokeMasters have developed a tool to help you on your quest to catch em' all. This program is called the PokeDex, a very powerful tool to help young trainers like yourself on their journey to becoming a Pokemon Master.

Not many know that there are over 800 known Pokemon in existence today, and with new ones being discovered every day, the PokeDex will help you get to know the vast world we share with this amazing creatures.

Logging your Pokemon is simple. Professor Oak has done most of the heavy lifting for you and loaded in all of the known Pokemon names (as their spelling can be a little confusing for young trainers). To begin, just start typing a Pokemon's name ...for example, let's use Pikachu.

Not only will the PokeDex tell you the spelling of its name, but it will also reveal its PokeDex entry number, attack, height, weight and Pokemon Type, in addition to what weather it performs best in and if it it currently weather boosted. Oh, and because Professor Oak and his team are the best there is, they have even provided you with pictures of their normal form and their extremely rare shiny form. (If you come across a shiny pokemon, do not hesitate to capture it).
